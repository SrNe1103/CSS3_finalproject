Immune System

The immune system is a network of cells, tissues, organs and substances that defend the body of infections and diseases (2025, NIH).

The immune cells are divided in different types that play different roles in the body.

For instance, the Dendritic Cells travel through the body, collecting what is called antigens. Then, they present those antigens to the T cells
